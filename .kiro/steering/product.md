# Product Overview

Abon Ikan SAKO is a landing page for a premium shredded fish (abon) product from Minahasa, North Sulawesi, Indonesia. The product is made by students at SMKN 2 Tondano vocational school in collaboration with local fishermen.

## Target Audience
- Indonesian families seeking healthy food options
- Health-conscious consumers
- Tourists looking for authentic Minahasa souvenirs (oleh-oleh)

## Core Brand Values
- Authentic (Otentik) - Traditional Minahasa recipes
- Hygienic (Higienis) - Produced in Teaching Factory standards
- Modern - Contemporary presentation and packaging
- Appetizing - Premium food photography aesthetic

## Language
All user-facing content is in Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia). The site uses `lang="id"` and locale `id_ID`.
